Our Projects & Programmes

Our programmes evolve from the needs of the people and communities with whom we work — rooted in listening, shaped by action.

The Lotus Project

Start the Adventure — offering opportunities for young people to connect, learn and grow together in a safe, welcoming, and fun environment. From group activities to one-to-one support, the Lotus Project is designed around what young people actually need.

YouthStart

Marie's Beacon Fund
