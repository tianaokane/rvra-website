In Memory of Marie McGuinness

Marie's Beacon Fund

Continuing Marie's legacy by supporting local groups who bring care, connection, and practical help to the Limavady community.

maries-beacon-fund.jpg
About the Fund

A small fund with a lasting community impact

Launched by RVRA and the McGuinness family, the Beacon Fund offers small community grants to help grassroots organisations improve lives across the Limavady area.

It was created to honour Marie's tireless commitment to her community — and to ensure her spirit of generosity, warmth, and kindness continues to shine.

Grant Information

Who the fund supports

Who Can Apply?

  • Small community groups based in or serving Limavady
  • Projects addressing isolation, poverty, or mental health
  • Initiatives focused on local development and connection
  • Groups with limited access to other funding streams

Key Details

  • Grants Available: Up to £500 per group
  • Deadline to Apply: 21 March 2025
  • Results Announced: 4 April 2025
  • Awarded By: RVRA & the McGuinness family

Marie's Legacy

Marie McGuinness was a beloved member of the RVRA family — known for her warmth, laughter, and unwavering belief that every person deserves kindness and support.

What Makes a Strong Application?

  • A clear community need
  • A practical idea that can be delivered locally
  • A focus on people who may otherwise feel isolated or unsupported
  • A simple explanation of how the grant will be used
