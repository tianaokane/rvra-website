Marie's Beacon Fund

In memory of Marie McGuinness, the Beacon Fund continues her legacy by supporting local groups making a real difference in our community.

About the Fund

Launched by RVRA Limavady and the McGuinness family, the Beacon Fund offers community grants to help grassroots organisations improve lives in the Limavady area.

Who Can Apply?

  • Small community groups based in or serving Limavady
  • Projects addressing isolation, poverty, mental health, or local development

Key Details

  • Grants Available: Up to £500 per group
  • Deadline to Apply: 21 March 2025
  • Results Announced: 4 April 2025
