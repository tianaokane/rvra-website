In Memory of Marie McGuinness

Marie's Beacon Fund

Launched by RVRA and the McGuinness family, the Beacon Fund continues Marie's legacy — supporting local groups who are making a real difference in our community.

maries-beacon-fund.jpg

About the Fund

The Beacon Fund offers community grants to help grassroots organisations improve lives in the Limavady area. It was created to honour Marie's tireless commitment to her community — and to ensure her spirit of generosity lives on.

Who Can Apply?

  • Small community groups based in or serving Limavady
  • Projects addressing isolation, poverty, or mental health
  • Initiatives focused on local development and connection
  • Groups with limited access to other funding streams

Key Details

  • Grants Available: Up to £500 per group
  • Deadline to Apply: 21 March 2025
  • Results Announced: 4 April 2025
  • Who Awards It: RVRA & the McGuinness family

Marie's Legacy

Marie McGuinness was a beloved member of the RVRA family — known for her warmth, her laughter, and her unwavering belief that every person deserves kindness and support. The Beacon Fund is her light, continuing to shine.
