What We Do

RVRA is all about building a stronger, safer, more connected Limavady — from grassroots support to youth empowerment, wellbeing, and lifelong learning.

Support

From crisis referrals to food assistance and mental health check-ins, we help individuals and families access the support they need.

Empowerment

We run inclusive programmes that build skills, grow confidence, and open doors — especially for young people and underrepresented voices.

Community

Our events, spaces, and projects are shaped by the people of Limavady. This is your community — and we’re here to support it together.
