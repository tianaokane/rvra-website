In Loving Memory
Marie McGuinness

Marie was a beloved part of the RVRA family — known for her warmth, her laughter, and her unwavering belief that every person deserves kindness and support.

We are proud to carry on her legacy through the Marie McGuinness Beacon Fund, supporting the causes she held closest to her heart.
