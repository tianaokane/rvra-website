In Loving Memory
Marie McGuinness

Marie was a beloved part of the RVRA family whose kindness, strength, and commitment to others left a lasting impact on everyone she worked with. She poured her heart into this community and inspired those around her to do the same.

We are proud to carry on her legacy through the Marie McGuinness Beacon Fund, supporting the causes she held closest to her heart.
