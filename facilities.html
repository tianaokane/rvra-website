Our Spaces

Community Facilities

Our spaces are open, welcoming, and ready to support a wide range of events, activities, and groups. Whether you're attending or booking — we've got space for you.

Available Rooms

What's Available

main-hall.jpg

Main Hall

Large, flexible space for events, classes, and community gatherings. Includes chairs, tables, and projector access. Capacity: up to 80 people.

meeting-room.jpg

Meeting Room

Perfect for workshops or small groups (up to 12 people) with whiteboard, screen, and good natural light. Fully accessible.

kitchen.jpg

Community Kitchen

Tea, coffee, and cooking space available for events and wellbeing sessions. Fully equipped and fully accessible. Ideal for catered events.

Room Booking

To enquire about room availability or make a booking, contact us. We offer affordable rates for local groups and charities.

Find Us

Getting Here

We have two community spaces in Limavady, both fully wheelchair accessible with convenient parking nearby.

Community House — 13–14 Keady Way

Address: 13–14 Keady Way, Limavady, BT49 0AU

By Bus: Routes 134, 135, and 138 stop nearby at Market Street

Parking: Free parking on Keady Way

Accessibility: Fully wheelchair accessible, ground floor access

Community Hub — Catherine Street

Address: Catherine Street, Limavady, BT49 0HQ

By Bus: Town centre routes — 5-minute walk from bus station

Parking: Town centre car parks within 3 minutes' walk

Accessibility: Fully wheelchair accessible, level access throughout

Empowering community in Limavady through inclusive support, creative programmes, and grassroots action.
