Empowering Community, Together

Rooted in Limavady, we bring people together to grow, support, and lead change — one project at a time.

The Roe Valley Residents Association (RVRA) is a community-led organisation based in Limavady. We work to empower residents of all ages through inclusive support programmes, advocacy, and collaborative projects that meet the real needs of the community.

Our Mission

To build a stronger, safer, and more connected community by delivering inclusive opportunities, nurturing potential, and championing social wellbeing for all.

Our Vision

A community where every individual feels valued, supported, and empowered to thrive — regardless of background, age, or ability.

Welcome from Karen
