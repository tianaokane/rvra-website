Our Organisation

Built by the community, for the community

The Roe Valley Residents Association (RVRA) is a community-led organisation based in Limavady. We work to empower residents of all ages through inclusive support programmes, advocacy, and collaborative projects that meet the real needs of our community.

From crisis support to celebration, from youth development to lifelong learning — RVRA is the place people turn to.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To build a stronger, safer, and more connected community by delivering inclusive opportunities, nurturing potential, and championing social wellbeing for all — regardless of background, age, or ability.

Our Vision

A community where every individual feels valued, supported, and empowered to thrive. Where no one is left behind, and where local voices drive real, lasting change.

Support

From crisis referrals to food assistance and mental health check-ins, we help individuals and families access what they need.

Empowerment

We run inclusive programmes that build skills, grow confidence, and open doors — especially for young people and underrepresented voices.

Community

Our events, spaces, and projects are shaped by the people of Limavady. This is your community — and we're here to support it together.

A Word From Our Project Manager

Welcome from Karen

Hello and welcome to the Roe Valley Residents Association. I'm Karen, and I'm proud to be part of a team that believes in the power of local voices and grassroots action.

Over the years, we've seen what happens when people come together to share ideas, listen to one another, and take action. RVRA was built on that belief — that everyone deserves to feel heard, included, and supported.

Whether you're here to learn more, get involved, or seek support — we're so glad you're here. We're always just a phone call or a knock on the door away.

— Karen, Project Manager
