Who We Are

A community centre built on care, connection, and action

The Roe Valley Residents Association is a community-led charity that has been the heartbeat of Limavady since 1998. We're the place people turn to — for support, for opportunity, and for each other.

From youth programmes to crisis support, from skills training to celebration — every part of what we do is shaped by the people we serve.
