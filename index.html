Who We Are

A community centre built on care, connection, and action

The Roe Valley Residents Association (RVRA) is a community-led charity based in Limavady. Since 1998 we've been the place people turn to — for support, for opportunity, and for each other.

From youth programmes to skills training, from crisis support to celebration, we're here for every chapter of community life.

Our Spaces

Community Facilities

Our spaces are open for events, training, support groups, and community gatherings.

Main Hall

Large flexible space for events, classes, and community gatherings. Includes chairs, tables, and projector access.

Meeting Room

Perfect for workshops or small groups (up to 12 people) with whiteboard and screen. Fully accessible.

Community Kitchen

Tea, coffee, and cooking space available for events and wellbeing sessions. Fully accessible.
