Supporting the Limavady Community

About Us

RVRA exists to improve quality of life for Limavady residents. We are committed to respect, inclusiveness, integrity, and equality in everything we do.

Our Services

We deliver support across education, health, environment, and social engagement. Our facilities include The Community House and the Youth & Community Hub.

For Children & Young People

  • Homework Support & Kids Klub
  • Drop-ins & Structured Programmes
  • Summer Schemes & Good Relations Projects

For Adults

  • Craft Clubs & Over-50s Social Group
  • Educational Classes
  • Good Relations Projects & Luncheon Clubs
